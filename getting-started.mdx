## Installation

Setting up Karmix Alchemist is straightforward and takes less than 10 minutes. Choose your preferred installation method based on your environment.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Cloud" icon="cloud">
    Start with our hosted solution for the fastest setup.
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
        Visit karmixalchemist.com and create your account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose Plan" icon="credit-card">
        Select a plan that fits your team's needs.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Setup Workspace" icon="settings">
        Configure your workspace settings and invite team members.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Self-Hosted" icon="server">
    Deploy on your own infrastructure for full control.
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="Docker,Docker Compose">
      ````bash
      docker run -d -p 3000:3000 karmix/alchemist:latest
      ````
      ````yaml
      version: '3.8'
      services:
        karmix-alchemist:
          image: karmix/alchemist:latest
          ports:
            - "3000:3000"
          environment:
            - DATABASE_URL=postgresql://user:pass@localhost:5432/karmix
      ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## First Project

Once installed, create your first documentation project to get familiar with the platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Project" icon="folder-plus">
    Click "New Project" and give it a descriptive name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Team Members" icon="users">
    Invite collaborators with appropriate permissions.
    
    <Callout kind="tip">Use role-based access control to manage who can edit, comment, or publish documents.</Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import Content" icon="upload">
    Import existing documentation from Markdown files or other sources.
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="CLI,API">
      ````bash
      karmix import --project my-project --source ./docs
      ````
      ````javascript
      const karmix = require('karmix-sdk');
      
      await karmix.projects.import({
        projectId: 'my-project',
        source: './docs',
        format: 'markdown'
      });
      ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Writing" icon="edit">
    Create your first document and begin documenting.
  </Step>
</Steps>
